About Us


We are a non-profit organization working to protect wildlife and restore natural ecosystems.

WildCare is a non-profit organization dedicated to protecting wildlife and preserving nature. We work closely with local communities, volunteers, and environmental experts to rescue injured animals, protect endangered species, and restore natural habitats.

Our mission is to create awareness, inspire action, and ensure a safe future for animals through education, rescue operations, and sustainable practices.

Our Mission

Animal Rescue

We rescue injured, abandoned, and abused animals and provide medical care, shelter, and rehabilitation before rehoming them safely.

Forest Protection

Our team works to protect forests by preventing illegal deforestation and promoting sustainable environmental practices.

Public Awareness

We conduct awareness programs, campaigns, and workshops to educate communities about wildlife conservation.
